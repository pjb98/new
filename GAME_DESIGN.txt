================================================================
  1. GAME OVERVIEW
================================================================

Chronic City is a browser-based top-down weed growing simulator
with Solana blockchain integration. Players start with nothing
but a borrowed house and a bag seed, build a grow operation from
the ground up, unlock new customers and locations, and eventually
run a full distribution network.

The core fantasy is "rags to riches" through hustle: growing
quality product, reading the market, managing supply and demand,
and expanding territory over time.

Target audience: Casual-to-midcore players who enjoy idle/sim
games, business sims (like Game Dev Tycoon, Drug Wars), and
crypto-native gaming audiences.

Platform: Browser (HTML5 / Phaser 3), hosted on GitHub Pages.
Wallet: Phantom (Solana) — optional, game is fully playable in
demo mode without a wallet.

  2. CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP
================================================================

  SHORT LOOP (minutes)
  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  Plant weed  →  Wait for growth  →  Harvest             │
  │  Walk to street  →  Knock on doors / find customers     │
  │  Sell stash  →  Earn cash  →  Buy upgrades              │
  └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  MEDIUM LOOP (hours / sessions)
  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  Accumulate enough cash  →  Unlock new strain           │
  │  Unlock better equipment  →  Grow faster / more yield  │
  │  Hit sales milestone  →  Unlock new customer / location │
  │  Unlock new grow spot  →  Scale production              │
  └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  LONG LOOP (days / weeks)
  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  Max out local block  →  Expand to new map area         │
  │  Build full roster of buyers  →  Run automated grows    │
  │  Earn SOL from on-chain sales  →  Buy NFT cosmetics     │
  │  Compete on leaderboards / flex stash                   │
  └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

SESSION FLOW
  1. Player opens game on street scene
  2. First time: choose a house as home base
  3. Enter home → plant current strain in grow setup
  4. Return to street while plant grows (real-time timer)
  5. Chat with / knock on doors of unlocked customers
  6. Return home when timer is up → harvest → go sell
  7. Use cash to buy better strains, equipment, locations
  8. Repeat with better yield and faster grows

  3. PROGRESSION SYSTEM
================================================================

STAGES OF PROGRESSION
─────────────────────
Stage 1 — The Plug (Start)
  • 1 home (chosen from 5 on starting block)
  • 1 customer unlocked (street wanderer)
  • Regular strain, Soil Pot setup
  • Earning ~$40–80 per harvest

Stage 2 — Getting Known (after first sale)
  • 2nd customer unlocked (door knocker)
  • Can afford OG Kush strain ($120)
  • Earning ~$80–150 per harvest

Stage 3 — Small Operation ($80 total earned)
  • 3rd customer unlocked
  • Can afford Hydro Kit ($300) → 40% faster grows
  • Earning ~$150–280 per harvest

Stage 4 — Mid-Level Dealer ($250 total earned)
  • 4th customer unlocked
  • Unlock Garage location ($400) → 2 plants at once
  • Blue Dream strain available ($250) → higher yield
  • Earning ~$300–500 per harvest

Stage 5 — Area Boss ($800+ total earned)
  • 5th customer unlocked (bulk buyer, lower per-gram price
    but takes 15–40g at once)
  • Warehouse location ($1200) → 4 plants
  • Girl Scout Cookies / Purple Haze strains available
  • Earning ~$600–1200 per cycle

Stage 6 — Kingpin ($3000+ total earned)
  • New map area unlocked (Downtown block)
  • LED Grow Tent ($600) → premium yields
  • White Widow / Gorilla Glue strains available
  • New NPC archetypes (restaurant owners, club promoters)
  • Earning ~$1500–3000 per cycle

UNLOCK TRIGGERS
  Milestone                Unlock
  ─────────────────────── ──────────────────────────────────
  Pick home house          1st street contact appears
  First completed sale     2nd contact (door knocker)
  $80 total earned         3rd contact
  $250 total earned        4th contact
  $800 total earned        5th contact (bulk buyer)
  $2000 total earned       2nd map area / Downtown
  $5000 total earned       Automated grow assistant NPC
  $10000 total earned      Black market broker (SOL sales)

REPUTATION SYSTEM (planned)
  Each NPC has a hidden reputation score 0–100.
  Selling regularly to them increases it.
  Higher rep → better prices, larger orders, exclusive strains.
  Stiffing a customer or selling bad product → rep loss.

ECONOMY BALANCE TARGETS
  The economy is tuned so that:
  • First upgrade (OG Kush) takes ~2–3 harvests
  • Players feel steady growth without feeling stuck
  • End-game items take real sessions of play (not grind)
  • There's always something attainable in the near future

CASH SINKS (where money goes)
  1. Strain seeds (one-time unlock, permanent)
  2. Equipment (one-time upgrade, replaces old)
  3. Locations (one-time unlock, stacks with previous)
  4. Cosmetics (pure flex, no gameplay impact)
  5. Planned: bribes to unlock new zones, repair events

INFLATION CONTROL
  As players progress, bigger customers buy more per transaction,
  preventing cash from accumulating too fast early. The market
  doesn't scale infinitely — top NPC demand caps at 60g/visit.

================================================================
  5. GROWING SYSTEM
================================================================

HOW IT WORKS
  1. Enter home → click the grow patch to plant
  2. Select active strain (from owned strains)
  3. Timer begins (real seconds, scaled by equipment/location)
  4. Growth shown as a visual progress bar + animated plant sprite
  5. Plant goes through 4 visual stages:
       Seedling → Vegetative → Mature → Flowering
  6. At 100% → HARVEST button appears → click to collect grams
  7. Yield = base strain yield × equipment mult × location mult

PLANT STAGES
  0–25%    Seedling      Small sprout, 2 leaves
  26–50%   Young Plant   3-leaflet fan leaves, branching
  51–75%   Mature Veg    5–7 leaflet fans, multiple branches
  76–100%  Flowering     Cola buds, amber pistils, trichomes,
                          glowing halo effect
  100%     Ready         Pulsing glow animation, harvest prompt

GROW TIMER FORMULA
  base_time × (1 / (equipment_speed × location_speed))

  Example: Gorilla Glue (120s base) + Auto-Farm (3x) + Lab (1.5x)
  = 120 / (3.0 × 1.5) = ~27 real seconds

MULTI-PLANT SYSTEM (planned, location-gated)
  Unlocking Garage+ gives additional plant slots.
  Each slot operates independently.
  UI shows all active plants with individual progress bars.

QUALITY SYSTEM (planned)
  Grows can produce Low / Mid / Top shelf product based on:
  • Equipment tier
  • Whether player tended the plant (manual interaction events)
  • Random chance (simulates real growing variance)
  Higher quality = higher price per gram.

EVENTS (planned, adds skill element)
  Random mid-grow events the player can respond to:
  • "Leaves yellowing" → spend $20 or lose 20% yield
  • "pH imbalance" → click to fix within 30s or lose quality
  • "Suspicious smell" → pay bribe or grow ends early

================================================================
  6. SALES & DISTRIBUTION
================================================================

STREET CUSTOMERS
  Unlocked customers wander the street.
  Walk up and click (or press E) → open sell dialog.
  They offer a randomized want (grams) and price ($/g).
  Player can accept or decline. NPCs stay on street for 2–3
  minutes before wandering away (planned).

DOOR KNOCKING
  Walk to a customer's house → E to Knock.
  NPC answers the door — dialog includes:
  • Flavor text / greeting
  • Their current buy offer
  Player cannot enter the house, only sell at the door.
  Same sell mechanic as street, different framing.

NPC DEMAND COOLDOWN (planned)
  Each NPC has a cooldown after buying (they need to restock too).
  Street customers: 3–5 minute cooldown
  Door knockers: 8–15 minute cooldown
  This prevents players from spamming one NPC.

PRICING NEGOTIATION (planned)
  With high enough reputation, players can counter-offer.
  Success chance based on rep score and charisma stat.

BULK DEALS (planned)
  Unlocked at Stage 5. High-volume buyers (Hector archetype)
  buy 20–60g but at lower $/g. Good for moving large stashes fast.

================================================================
  8. SHOP & UPGRADES
================================================================

SHOP TABS
  Strains    — unlock new seed strains (permanent)
  Equipment  — upgrade grow setup (replaces, not stacks)
  Locations  — unlock more grow space (stacks / expands)
  Cosmetics  — visual-only home decorations

HOW TO ACCESS
  Press B anywhere on the street or in your home.
  While the shop is open, click-to-move is disabled.

SHOP UX
  Each item shows: Name, Description, Price, Buy button.
  Owned items are grayed out with "Owned" label.
  Items too expensive are still visible but button is disabled.
  Price shown in $ (cash). Planned: some items cost SOL.

UPGRADE PHILOSOPHY
  Equipment and location upgrades are permanent and meaningful.
  No pay-to-win — all progression is achievable through gameplay.
  The biggest upgrades should feel like real milestones.

PLANNED SHOP ADDITIONS
  • Consumables: Fertilizer (one-time yield boost), Fans (temp
    speed boost), pH kit (prevents quality loss events)
  • Black Market tab (unlocked late-game): rare seeds, equipment
    from "off the books" suppliers
  • NFT Cosmetics tab (Solana wallet required): limited edition
    room decorations, character skins, pet accessories

================================================================
  9. MAP & LOCATION EXPANSION
================================================================

CURRENT MAP — THE BLOCK
  • 5 houses on a single street
  • 1 is player's home, 4 are customer houses
  • Street with sidewalk, curb, road, fountain plaza
  • Trees, decorative elements

PLANNED MAP ZONES (unlocked via progression)
  Zone 1 — The Block (Starting area)
    Residential street, 4–5 customers, basic economy

  Zone 2 — Downtown ($2000+ earned, planned)
    Commercial district, new NPCs (club, restaurant)
    Higher prices, more buyers, higher heat risk
    Larger map with new landmarks

  Zone 3 — The Warehouse District ($8000+ earned, planned)
    Industrial zone, big bulk buyers
    Warehouse grow locations available to purchase
    Rival gang presence (competition mechanics)

  Zone 4 — The Hills ($20000+ earned, planned)
    Upscale neighborhood, luxury buyers
    Dispensary partner unlockable
    Premium-only product market

TRAVEL BETWEEN ZONES (planned)
  A taxi icon or bus stop on the edge of the map.
  Costs $10 per trip (small cash sink).
  Player returns to same zone position on next visit.

GROW LOCATION RELATIONSHIP
  Growing locations expand as zones unlock:
  Bedroom → Garage → Warehouse → Greenhouse → Secret Lab
  Each step unlocks when you purchase the location item in shop
  AND have reached the right progression stage.

  10. RETENTION MECHANICS
================================================================

SESSION HOOKS (why players come back)
  ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  "My plant is ready to harvest"                        │
  │  The grow timer gives players a reason to return at a  │
  │  specific future time. Real-time growth means the game │
  │  is always doing something even when closed.           │
  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  "I'm almost at the next unlock"                       │
  │  Progression thresholds are tuned so there's always    │
  │  a meaningful goal ~10–30 minutes of play away.        │
  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  "I want to see what's behind that locked house"       │
  │  Locked doors with visible unlock requirements keep    │
  │  the street itself as a persistent curiosity hook.     │
  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

DAILY ENGAGEMENT (planned)
  • Daily login bonus — small cash reward for logging in
  • Daily customer — a roaming NPC with 2x price offer,
    appears once per day for 10 minutes
  • Streak multiplier — 5-day streak doubles yield for a day

SOCIAL HOOKS (planned)
  • Public leaderboard — top earners by week / all-time
  • "Flex wall" — players can share their home setup as a
    screenshot to social platforms
  • Referral system — invite a friend, both get $100 bonus cash

VARIABLE REWARD (built into current loop)
  Customer prices and demand are randomized within ranges.
  This slot-machine effect keeps each sale slightly surprising.
  Players are always curious: will this NPC pay more today?

PROGRESSION VISIBILITY
  HUD shows: SOL balance, Cash, Current stash (grams)
  Grow panel shows: Strain name, growth %, harvest button
  These are always visible — player always knows their state
  and what they're working toward.

LOSS PREVENTION (sunk cost engagement)
  Once a player has a plant growing, they feel compelled to
  return to harvest it. A mid-grow investment creates a
  powerful return trigger.

PLANNED: Decay & Urgency
  Harvested plants not collected within a time window lose
  potency (quality drops). Adds urgency without being punishing.

PLANNED: Rival NPCs
  A competing dealer (Dimitri, Rico) appears periodically
  and "steals" one of your customers if you haven't sold to them
  recently. Creates urgency to maintain client relationships.

  11. SOLANA / WALLET INTEGRATION
================================================================

CURRENT STATE
  • Phantom wallet connect button on splash screen
  • Demo mode available (no wallet required)
  • SOL balance displayed in HUD when connected
  • No on-chain transactions yet

PLANNED BLOCKCHAIN FEATURES

  Real SOL Transactions
  • High-value late-game sales can optionally settle in SOL
  • Exchange rate: $50 in-game = 0.001 SOL (variable)
  • Player can choose: take cash or SOL for each sale
  • Actual Solana transfer to/from wallet (Devnet first)

  NFT Cosmetics
  • Limited edition home decorations minted as NFTs
  • Seasonal drops (e.g., "Summer Harvest" collection)
  • Trade cosmetics with other players on secondary market
  • Rare items provide small stat bonuses (5% yield, etc.)

  On-Chain Leaderboard
  • Weekly competition — most grams harvested
  • Top 3 players receive small SOL prize pool
  • Proof of score verified on-chain

  Strain NFTs (planned, premium)
  • Legendary strains available only as NFT purchases
  • Carry across seasons / resets
  • Can be traded, sold, or rented to other players

WALLET-GATED CONTENT
  • Free-to-play base game accessible to all (demo mode)
  • Wallet connection unlocks: SOL balance display, NFT shop
  • Real SOL transactions: wallet + small devnet test required
  • Philosophy: wallet enhances, never gates core gameplay

  13. PLANNED FUTURE SYSTEMS
================================================================

HEAT SYSTEM
  Growing and selling attracts attention. Heat meter 0–100.
  High heat: police NPC patrols appear, random busts possible.
  Lower heat: lay low (don't sell for 30 min), pay bribes.
  Adds risk/reward tension to high-volume play.

RIVAL DEALERS
  After Stage 4, a rival NPC (Dimitri / other names) appears.
  Competes for the same customers. Steals buyers you neglect.
  Can be dealt with: outcompete them (better product), bribe
  them to leave, or in later stages — partner with them.

QUALITY TIERS
  Low / Mid / Top / Exotic shelf grades.
  Determined by strain, equipment, and care events.
  Some customers only buy specific tiers (Mia = top shelf only).
  Price premium: Top = +30%, Exotic = +80% base rate.

CRAFTING / PROCESSING (planned)
  Process raw grams into:
  • Edibles (higher value, different customer market)
  • Concentrates (smaller g quantity, much higher $/g)
  • Pre-rolls (bulk sales, lower price, volume play)
  Each product needs a processing item from the shop.

MULTIPLAYER / CO-OP (long-term)
  • See other players' characters on shared street scene
  • Trade product peer-to-peer
  • Form crews to take over zones together
  • Crew hideout as a shared space

MOBILE PORT
  • Responsive layout for phones (vertical scroll view)
  • Touch-friendly UI (larger buttons, swipe to move)
  • Push notifications for "harvest ready"

SEASONAL EVENTS
  • 4/20 Festival — 2x prices for 24 hours
  • Harvest Moon — faster grow speeds for a week
  • Winter — new snowy map skin, holiday NPCs
  • Summer — outdoor grow mechanic unlocked temporarily
